Blue Flag Beaches in Greece

Shiny Application

Story -about

The application presents the Blue Flag Beaches in Greece on a map, while at the same time providing basic statistics data at real time dynamically environment.

Components

  • Selection boxes
  • Geographical map
  • Plots
  • Table

Data

Data are from Geoadata.gov.gr
for the years between 2008 and 2010.
